The Process

From photo to
perfect lawn

Three steps is all it takes. No equipment, no soil samples, no expertise required.

1
Upload a photo of your lawn

Take any photo on your phone — wide shot, patchy area, or a section you're worried about. Snap it in natural daylight for the best results. No special equipment needed.

2
Add a few optional details

Tell us your grass type (or let the AI guess), any recent treatments like watering or fertilizing, and any specific concerns. The more context, the more personalized the analysis.

3
Get your personalized care plan

Within seconds, receive a full health score, scientific breakdown, plain-language tips, product recommendations tailored to your lawn's exact issues, and a 7-day action plan.
